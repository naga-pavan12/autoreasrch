# Company Goals

## Primary Business Goal
Increase adoption among mid-market B2B support teams by helping them resolve customer issues faster and with more confidence.

## Product Goals
1. Reduce average time-to-resolution.
2. Reduce manual triage and repetitive agent work.
3. Improve trust in AI-assisted workflows.
4. Help teams go live quickly without requiring heavy setup or consultants.

## Strategic Focus
- AI-assisted support operations
- Mid-market teams
- Fast time-to-value
- Reliable automation over flashy but fragile AI

## Success Signals
- Faster ticket routing and prioritization
- Less context switching for agents
- Higher automation adoption
- Lower setup complexity
- Better manager trust in AI recommendations

## Non-Goals
- Building for highly customized enterprise-only workflows
- Services-heavy implementations
- Broad “all-in-one CRM” positioning
