# Constraints

## Product Constraints
- Avoid deep enterprise customization as the main wedge.
- Avoid opportunities requiring large service teams.
- Prefer fast onboarding and simple configuration.
- Prefer explainable workflows over black-box automation.

## Research Constraints
- Do not treat a single quote as a broad market truth.
- Separate symptoms from root causes when possible.
- Prefer evidence-backed insights over clever summaries.
- Rank opportunities based on both user pain and strategic fit.

## Evaluation Preferences
A better discovery output is:
- specific
- grounded in quotes or evidence
- not repetitive
- strategically relevant
- useful for PM decision-making
