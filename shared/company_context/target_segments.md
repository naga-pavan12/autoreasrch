# Target Segments

## Primary Segment
Mid-market B2B SaaS support teams with 20 to 300 agents.

## Secondary Segment
Support operations managers and CX leaders responsible for workflow design, triage quality, and team productivity.

## Key Users
- Support agents
- Support managers
- Support operations managers
- CX leaders

## Typical Environment
- Multiple support channels
- Growing ticket volume
- Some automation already in place
- Limited dedicated ops or admin resources
- High pressure to improve resolution speed without adding headcount
