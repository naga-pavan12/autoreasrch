# Product Strategy

We aim to win by solving real operational pain in support teams, not by matching every feature competitors advertise.

## Strategic Principles
1. Focus on painful, repeated workflows.
2. Prefer narrower but more reliable AI assistance.
3. Make automation understandable and easy to trust.
4. Reduce setup burden for mid-market teams.
5. Use evidence-backed problem discovery to guide what we build.

## What Strong Opportunities Look Like
- Frequently observed across multiple sources
- Painful enough to change buying behavior
- Closely tied to support team efficiency
- Poorly solved by current competitors
- Credible for our team to solve well

## What Weak Opportunities Look Like
- Nice-to-have requests without real pain
- Rare edge cases
- Problems only large enterprises care about
- Areas where we lack strategic fit
